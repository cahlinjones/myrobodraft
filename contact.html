FAQ

Common Questions

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed in a single day. We'll set up the RTK base station, map your property, configure all settings, and train you on the app before we leave.

Do I need to be home for the installation?

Yes, we require someone 18 or older to be present during installation. We'll walk you through the system operation and answer all your questions before we leave.

What if I have problems after installation?

We provide ongoing support via phone and email. Premium maintenance packages include 24/7 emergency service and priority response times.

Can I see a demo before I buy?

Absolutely! We can arrange an on-site demonstration at your property or at our location. Contact us to schedule a demo.

Will the snow blower work on gravel driveways?

Yes! The Yarbo snow blower is designed to work effectively on gravel driveways. It features adjustable height settings that allow it to clear snow while staying above the gravel surface, preventing damage to your driveway while still providing excellent snow removal.
