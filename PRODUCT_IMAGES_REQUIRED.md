## Image Sources

### Manufacturer Websites
1. **Yarbo**: https://yarbo.com
2. **Mammotion**: https://mammotion.com
3. **Echo**: https://echo-robotics.com (or Echo Power Equipment)
4. **Pudu Robotics**: https://www.pudurobotics.com
5. **Sveaverken**: https://sveaverken.com (or search for Thor mower)
6. **Sveabot**: https://sveabot.com

### Tips for Getting Images
- Download high-res images from manufacturer press kits
- Use manufacturer product pages (right-click → Save Image)
- Contact manufacturers for marketing materials
- Use online image converters to convert to WebP format
- Resize images appropriately (800x600 for main, 200x200 for thumbnails)

### Converting to WebP
Use online tools like:
- https://cloudconvert.com/jpg-to-webp
- https://convertio.co/jpg-webp/
- Or use Photoshop/GIMP with WebP plugin
